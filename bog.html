B O G

Board of Governors

This is the executive committee of PG Association. It consists of a Staff Advisor,a staff treasurer, PG Representative, Year wise coordinators, Convenors of 4 councils will constitute the Board of Governors. The total strength will be 28. BoG will have 5 main administrative posts i.e, President, Vice President, General Secretary, Treasurer ,Convenor and they will by default become the post holders of PG Association. BoG discuss matters related to PG Association’s working and take decisions upon that if supported by the majority of members.

About Us

PG Association is a group of around 1300 PG students currently pursuing their course at CET. It is formed under the leadership of the PG Representative of College Union. It’s vision is to promote the interests of PG students and support technical, research, academic, social, cultural and job oriented activities of the students.
